<!-- PRD-008 §5 World Definition -->

Time:
A single summer, 20 days long. One turn is one day.

Setting:
A quiet suburban street corner. The player runs one stand alone.

Economy:
All prices are in whole cents. There is no inflation, no competitors,
no taxes.

Physics:
Ice melts overnight. Lemons and sugar do not spoil during the game window.

Culture:
Customers are anonymous; the game does not model individuals, only
aggregate demand.
